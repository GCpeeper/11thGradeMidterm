White Water

White water or rapids are a place where a river narrows or constricts. This causes complex currents and large waves. There are many sports that utilizes these rivers and white water. Some examples are White Water Rafting, White Water SUPing(stand up paddleboarding). Many of these sports are considered extreme sports, however much of that is based on the river and the rapid. There are a lot of different rivers, in the world each has a different style of white water, becuase of how it formed. This allows for many rivers to not be extreme: Making a river for almost everybody.
